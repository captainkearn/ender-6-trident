## Print Settings Baseline

Recommended material for structural parts:

- ASA or ABS
- 4 walls minimum
- 40% infill minimum
- 5 top/bottom layers minimum

Final print settings should follow the source model recommendations when using known Voron or Dragon Burner ecosystem parts.
